Sree Swami Vaidhya Gurukulam

Sree Swami Vaidhya Gurukulam, situated in Perumbavoor, Ernakulam (Dist.) of Kerala is the representation of a unique perspective in the way that healthy living is visualized and actualized, in a manner far ahead of our times. By taking a step back to study the age old and nearly forgotten traditions that had gifted our ancestors with long and peace filled lives, the methodology of life preached and practiced here aims at gifting health back to the people, not just though Ayurvedic treatment methods, but also though training, research and by spreading awareness.
